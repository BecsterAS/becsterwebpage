About Us

Founded in March 2023, Becster Aps specializes in advanced microwave non-destructive testing (NDT) of composite materials. Leveraging cutting-edge microwave technology, we offer faster, non-contact inspection solutions that provide superior defect detection compared to traditional ultrasound methods. So far, we have worked with Danish industry leaders in the wind turbine blade sector, where our patent pending technology has shown its ability to identify multiple defects within complex structures, ensuring higher quality and safety standards. Becster was founded by four graduates from Aalborg University specializing in electronic systems, mechanical engineering, and software.
